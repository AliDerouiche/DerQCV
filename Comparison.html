The Future of Cryptography

As quantum computing advances, classical cryptographic systems like RSA face increasing threats. Quantum cryptography protocols like BB84 represent the future of secure communication, offering unconditional security based on the fundamental laws of physics rather than computational complexity.

While RSA remains widely used and effective for current applications, the transition to quantum-resistant cryptography is essential for long-term security in the quantum era.
