What is the BB84 Protocol?

The BB84 protocol is the first quantum key distribution protocol, invented in 1984 by Charles Bennett and Gilles Brassard. It allows two parties (Alice and Bob) to generate a shared secret key using quantum states.

The principle relies on the fact that measuring a quantum state disturbs that state. If an eavesdropper (Eve) attempts to intercept the communication, her presence will be detected by the disturbances she introduces.
